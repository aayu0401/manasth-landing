Platform Capabilities

A clinical picture — ranked, prioritised, actionable.

01 — Risk Stratification

We surface who needs attention — and how much.

Our AI processes multimodal signals across brain health, lifestyle, and clinical domains to assign each individual a precise risk tier. Not a score. A clinical picture — ranked, prioritised, actionable.

02 — Clinical Triaging

We determine the right next step, automatically.

Not every brain health concern requires a neurologist. Our triaging engine directs patients to the right level of care — GP, specialist, coordinator, or urgent pathway — reducing noise for clinicians.

03 — Care Coordination

We close the gap between assessment and action.

Identification without follow-through is a missed opportunity. Manasth connects risk stratification to structured care pathways — keeping patients in the system and outcomes measurable.
